Black Bean Fajita Skillet



Description


This 3-Ingredient Black Bean Fajita Skillet is a culinary chameleon, ready to transform into whatever quick, satisfying meal you need! With just black beans, pre-sliced fajita vegetables, and Southwest or taco seasoning, this dish comes together in a flash. Saut√© the vibrant peppers and onions until they're tender-crisp, then toss in the seasoned black beans, letting them soak up all that zesty flavor. Whether you tuck it into warm tortillas, pile it high on a bed of rice, or use it as a hearty base for a vibrant salad, this skillet is a testament to how delicious simplicity can be.


Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1-2 cups pre-sliced fajita vegetables (onions, bell peppers)
  • 1 packet (or 2 tablespoons) Southwest or taco seasoning
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • (Optional) Tortillas or cooked riced for serving

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the pre-sliced fajita vegetables and saute for 5-7 minutes, until tender crisp.
  3. Stir in black beans and seasoning. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through and well combined.
  4. Serve immediately with warm tortillas or over cooked rice.
